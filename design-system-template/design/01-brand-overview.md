# 1. Brand Overview

Warm editorial design. Cream canvas `#f5f3ed`, corporate red `#762224` for CTAs only, warm-toned neutrals throughout. Anthropic type family: Serif (headlines 500), Sans (body), Mono (code).

**Logo:** `#762224` on light, `#fffcf8` on dark. SVG in `/public/images/`.

**Themes:** `samsung-one-ui` (base) | `one-group` (brand overlay, default)
