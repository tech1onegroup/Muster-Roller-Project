# 8. Rules

## Do
Canvas `#f5f3ed` | Serif 500wt headlines | `#762224` CTAs only | warm neutrals | ring shadows | `#d4453a` errors | serif/sans hierarchy | body LH 1.60 | radius 12-32px

## Don't
`#762224` for errors | cool grays | bold 700+ on Serif | extra saturated colors | sharp <6px corners | Anthropic fonts publicly | hardcoded hex in components | modify layout in Strict Skin Mode | `#ffffff` page bg | body LH <1.40

## Error vs Brand
`#762224` (brand) and `#d4453a` (error) must be visually distinct. Never use brand red for error states.
